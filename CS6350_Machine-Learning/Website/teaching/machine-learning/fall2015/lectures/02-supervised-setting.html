Supervised Learning: The setup

←Previous lecture  |  Home  |  Next lecture →

In this lecture, we will look at the general setting of supervised learning. We will start by continuing the badges game. Then, we will spend the rest of the lecture defining the various spaces associated with supervised learning: instance space, label space, hypothesis space. We will look at examples of how the choice of hypothesis space is very important.
